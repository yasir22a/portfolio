🙋 About Me

🔧 Why Mechatronics?

Mechatronics combines everything I enjoy: logic, creativity, and practical impact. It brings together coding, circuits, machines, and systems into one thrilling discipline.

I chose Mechatronics because I didn’t want to limit myself. I wanted to build robots, automate homes, solve problems, and dream in both wires and logic!

Mechatronics is the art of engineering for the curious mind. It’s where code meets the physical world, and where I find my purpose.
